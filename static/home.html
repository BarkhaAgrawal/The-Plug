About Us

"The Plug", a startup created by founder Quinton Brown in March 2020. Some of the most iconic companies of his childhood were created during the great recession (2008-2009) such as Uber, Airbnb, Slack, WhatsApp etc. Amid the Covid-19 outbreak Quinton set out to create a website that highlights top trending brands. His love of fashion and and passion for helping others fueled what we now know as "The Plug".
